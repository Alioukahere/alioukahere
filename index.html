why me

I like solving problems, it's my everyday job. With 2 years of experience, my intellectual curiosity pushed me to find answers to the most relevant questions. Whether it's finding a design that fits the customer's needs, or writing the perfect algorithm, I was able to quickly identify a customer's problem and find a strategic solution.

Web Full Stack Developer, hardworking, prolific, I create elegant applications on both design (UI / UX) and on architecture using modern and evolutive technologies (Python, Django, Flask, PHP, Symfony, React JS, React Native, Node JS, Express JS, Sass). Self-taught, curious and rigorous, I adapt in all circumstances.

My mission: To train the maximum number of young Africans in computer programming. I spent the last year supporting and introducing young people in Dakar and Conakry to web development. I am more than convinced that Africa's development will be driven by ICT and Africa can be the global developer provider.

My goal: To prepare a generation of creative African developers.
